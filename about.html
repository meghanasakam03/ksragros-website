Company overview :

KSR Agricultural Implements & Works is a leading manufacturer and Trader in agricultural machinery and implements, with a strong presence in sales and service across Andhra Pradesh and Telagana State with foot prints in Karanataka and Tamilnadu . SUN FLOWER:

Sunflowers are processed into cooking oil, meal and confectionary products. Distinct varieties are used for oil and for confectionary purposes. Meal is a byproduct of the oil is extraction process and is used primarily as an ingredient in livestock feed rations.

PADDY:

Paddy processing: Refers to the industrial process of turning raw paddy (rice) into consumable rice products. This includes companies that mill paddy into rice, and may also be involved in value-added products like rice bran oil.

MILLETS:

Millets are a group of small-seeded cereal grasses that are a staple food in many parts of Asia and Africa, known for being drought-tolerant and nutrient-dense. They are rich in protein, fiber, vitamins, and minerals, naturally gluten-free, and are used to make flatbreads, porridge, or are cooked and eaten like rice. Popular types include pearl millet (Bajra), finger millet (Ragi), and foxtail millet (Kangni).

RED GRAM:

Yes, red gram and toor dal are the same thing; they are just different names for the split pigeon pea, also known as arhar dal. "Toor dal" is the common name in southern India, while "arhar dal" is used more frequently in the north, but they all refer to the same legume. .
